Hi! I am a J.D./Ph.D. student at Stanford Law School and Stanford Political Science. I graduated as valedictorian from Dartmouth College in 2022 with a triple major in Government, Environmental Studies, and Quantitative Social Science. My empirical research has appeared in journals including <i>Nature Communications</i> and <i>Energy Research & Social Science</i>. My legal research has appeared in the <i>Stanford Law Review</i> and the <i>University of Illinois Law Review</i>.
